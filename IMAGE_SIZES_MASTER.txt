================================================================================
Use these production export sizes. Format: WIDTHxHEIGHT (aspect ratio).

Each entry lists:
  • Production size (what to export)
  • Storage path (under storage/app/public → URL /storage/…)
  • Page / section
  • Frontend display (CSS; may crop with object-fit: cover)

K) EXPORT RULES FOR DESIGNER
================================================================================

    Quality:        80–85% JPEG/WebP for photos
    Color profile:  sRGB
    File types:
      • Photos / banners:     JPG or WebP
      • Logos (transparency): PNG or WebP
    Cropping:       Heroes and cards use object-fit: cover — keep subject in center
                    safe zones listed above for text overlays
    Retina:         Production widths above are minimums; 2x exports optional for logos
    Max upload:     Portal hero 8 MB; most admin images 5 MB (server validation)
